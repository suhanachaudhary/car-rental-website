about us

MyChoize Self Drive Cars is a self drive brand owned by ORIX, Japan’s second largest self drive car rental company currently managing more than 63,000 cars in our fleet in Japan. With MyChoize, we endeavor to provide Indian users the Best in World Japanese service and technology at Indian prices. MyChoize is currently present in 16 Indian cities including Bangalore, Pune, Mumbai, Delhi-NCR, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Indore, Chandigarh, Amritsar, Dehradun, Rishikesh, Haridwar.and Udaipur.

In India, ORIX is already the largest B2B Car Rental player in India, and having captured the B2B chauffeur driven car market, entering the self-driven car market was just a matter of time for us. It all started in 2016 when we at ORIX started receiving requests from users like yourself who wanted our world class maintained cars but also demanded the privacy of only their loved ones. We felt that there is a strong need to address this situation, and MyChoize Self Drive cars thus became our answer for this need of our users. And now that we are here, you don’t need to look anywhere else for your need of self drive car rentals – be it hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, MUVs or luxury cars.
